# GA4 vs SealMetrics FAQ

## Why are GA4 and SealMetrics numbers different?

Because GA4 relies on cookies, consent, and session-based tracking.  
SealMetrics tracks isolated hits without consent and without cookies.

GA4 loses 50 to 85 percent of data when people reject cookies.  
SealMetrics measures 100 percent of traffic.

---

## Why is GA4's direct traffic inflated?

If a user accepts cookies mid-journey, GA4 loses attribution and assigns the visit to "Direct".  
SealMetrics attributes hits correctly because it does not depend on consent.

---

## Why does SealMetrics count more conversions than GA4?

Because GA4 cannot track conversions from visitors who reject consent.  
SealMetrics can.

---

## Why compare SealMetrics clicks to GA4 sessions?

When a visitor returns after leaving, GA4 merges visits into one session.  
SealMetrics counts hits independently, as required by privacy regulations.
